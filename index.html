About Steve Jobs

Steven Paul Jobs (1955–2011) was an American entrepreneur, industrial designer, and visionary leader. He was the co-founder of Apple Inc. and played a key role in revolutionizing the personal computer, smartphone, music, and animation industries.

Major Achievements

  • Co-founded Apple Inc. in 1976
  • Introduced the Macintosh in 1984
  • Revolutionized music with the iPod & iTunes
  • Led the creation of the iPhone and iPad
  • CEO of Pixar Animation Studios
  • Transformed Apple into one of the world’s most valuable companies

Famous Quote

“Your time is limited, so don’t waste it living someone else’s life.”

Legacy

Steve Jobs’ legacy lives on through Apple’s innovative products and design philosophy. His focus on simplicity, creativity, and user experience continues to shape modern technology.
